Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2024

At DopamineDash, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our dopamine-friendly task management mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Task Data: The tasks, categories, streak data, and content you create within the app are stored on your device. This is your personal data, and you retain full ownership and control.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect non-personal usage data to understand how features are used and to improve the app. This includes app launches, timer usage, task completion rates, and crash reports.
  • Device Information: We collect limited device information including your device type, operating system version, and app version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Subscription Information: If you purchase DopamineDash Premium, subscription information is handled securely through Apple's in-app purchase system. We do not directly process credit card or payment information.
  • Notification Preferences: Your notification settings and reminder preferences are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide the core features of DopamineDash, including task creation, timer functionality, streak tracking, and notifications.
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve the app's performance, features, and user experience, particularly focusing on the 5-minute timer effectiveness and user engagement.
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries about the app's features and functionality.
  • Bug Fixes: To identify and fix technical issues, crashes, and timer-related problems.
  • Features: To develop new features based on user needs, such as additional timer durations, new categories, and enhanced streak tracking.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your task data, streak information, and timer history are primarily stored on your device. This ensures your privacy and gives you complete control over your information.
  • No Cloud Storage: DopamineDash does not store your tasks on our servers unless you opt in to cloud features (if available in future updates).
  • Encryption: We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
  • No Selling or Sharing: We never sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide and improve our dopamine-friendly task management service.

4. Subscription and Payment Information

  • Apple App Store Payments: DopamineDash Premium subscription payments ($2.99 weekly, $9.99 monthly, $49.99 yearly) are processed through Apple's in-app purchase system. Apple handles all payment processing, billing, and subscription management.
  • No Direct Payment Processing: DopamineDash does not collect, store, or process credit card information directly.
  • Subscription Data: Your subscription status is used to determine which features you have access to within the app (unlimited tasks, custom categories, etc.).
  • Apple Privacy: Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for information on how your payment information is protected.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your task data, streak information, and timer history within the app at any time.
  • Delete: You can delete individual tasks, categories, or your entire account. To delete your account and all associated data, email support@dopaminedash.app with "Account Deletion Request" as the subject.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics collection by disabling it in your app settings.
  • Do Not Track: If your device has a "Do Not Track" setting, DopamineDash respects this preference.
  • Notification Control: You can manage notification preferences directly in your device settings and within the app.

6. Children's Privacy

DopamineDash is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will immediately delete such information. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@dopaminedash.app.

7. Third-Party Services

DopamineDash may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services may collect anonymized usage data. We do not share your personal task data, streak information, or timer history with these services.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" and posting the revised policy in the app and on our website. Your continued use of DopamineDash constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@dopaminedash.app
  • Current Support: docs-blue-theta.vercel.app/support
  • Subject: Privacy Inquiry
  • Response Time: Within 30 days

10. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store privacy requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
